  (defun describe-hash-descend (hash)
    "Recursive describe hash func for nested hash-tables - mangled"
    (maphash (lambda (key value)
               (pp key) (princ " => ")
               (if (hash-table-p value)
                     (describe-hash-descend value))
             hash))

I’m an editor of the EmacsWiki . I invite all Emacs and XEmacs users to take up editing responsibilities as well. All you need to do is edit new pages, correct typos, add categories, rework pages. A good rule of thumb I try to follow is to make all pages potential sections in a printed book or info manual.

I usually don’t sign my contributions because I think that encourages other people to rework the page. Signing contributions would not make sense in a traditional book, either.
